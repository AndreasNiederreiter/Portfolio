PET THE CATS

In the third semester, I developed as a study project the story game "Pet the Cats" with a group of four. The player character Kallisto is new in the little, peaceful town, but after some days and some talks with other residents, they find out that something isn't right. One of the main ideas was that the game starts in a peaceful, beautiful, and cute mood, but that it changes dramatically over time into a darker, more brutal, unpleasant, and sad vibe. We had the idea to slowly change every colour in the game over time into a colder, uglier palette to better transfer the mood switch to the player. I did the rough sketch of the story, some game design, and all of the art.

This isn't a trailer. It's only a short showcase of the gameplay so you can get an impression of the game.
