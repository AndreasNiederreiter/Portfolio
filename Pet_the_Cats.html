PET THE CATS

Pet the Cats is a small 3D game I created in the first semester as a university project. You play as a cute bear and explore a small town to find and pet cats. The goal is to collect all the cats and interact with them. I was responsible for the entire art, animation, and level design.

This isn't a trailer. It's only a little showcase of the gameplay, so you can get an impression of the game.
