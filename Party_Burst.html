PARTY BURST

Within the framework of the Global Game Jam 2025, I developed the game "Party Burst" together with a group. It’s a visual novel set at a party. You talk with different participants and quickly notice that fascist ideas are spreading. You have to make statements during the discussions, and your counterpart reacts accordingly. I designed the character Maze and his corresponding reactions.

Note: This trailer is an early preview - the game is not finished yet. I did not edit the trailer; I'm sharing it to give an impression of the game.
